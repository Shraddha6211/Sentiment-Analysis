How It Works
STEP 01
Text Cleaning
Remove URLs, @mentions, symbols. Lowercase everything.
STEP 02
TF-IDF
Convert words to numbers. Rare-but-frequent words score highest.
STEP 03
Logistic Regression
Multiply weights × features. Softmax converts to probabilities.
STEP 04
Prediction
Highest probability class wins: positive, negative, or neutral.
